My Work

Manual Mill and Lathe

I made a high tolerance hammer using only manual mills and lathes. This project taught me skills like angled cutting on a mill and single pointing on a lathe.

Conversational Mill and Fixture Design

Due to warpage from welding, I had to refinish the bearing surfaces of the trailing arm. I designed and manufactured a custom fixture to hold the part while I used a conversational mill to remake the surfaces and snap ring groove.

CNC Mill

For the Happy Cupcake project, I helped create the CAM programs in NX and helped run the CNC machine. We made a two part mold for our injection molded parts.

General Manufacturing

In my time on the Baja team, I have helped with the manufacturing of dozens of parts and jigs. I have gained experience with everything from sheet metal to carbon fiber layups to welding.
